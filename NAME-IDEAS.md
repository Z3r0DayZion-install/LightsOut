# 🏷️ Naming Ideas for Modular Sleep Timer System

## The Core Philosophy
Two separate apps that work together:
- **Timer App:** Precise, reliable, focused on timing
- **RGB App:** Visual, creative, focused on display

---

## 🎯 Option 1: Functional Names (Clear & Professional)

### Timer App Names
1. **ChronoCore** - "Chrono" (time) + "Core" (foundation)
2. **TempusEngine** - Latin "time" + engine
3. **TickCore** - Simple, memorable
4. **SleepTimer-Core** - Descriptive, keeps brand
5. **PowerNap** - User-friendly, catchy
6. **TimeKeeper** - Classic, trustworthy

### RGB App Names

5. **KeyLight** - Keyboard + light

**Pairing:** `ChronoCore` + `ChromaFlow`

## 📝 Name Selection Guide

### Choose ChronoCore + ChromaFlow if:
- You want professional/enterprise appeal
- You're targeting power users & IT admins
- You want consistent branding

### Choose Dozr + GlowGrid if:
- You want broad consumer appeal
- You're building a product brand
- Social media/marketing is important

### Choose Tempus + LuxIO if:
- You want sophisticated technical appeal
- International/multilingual audience
- Developer-focused positioning

- **Marketing:** Dozr (catchy)

- **Package:** Pro/Suite for combined
